How money moves

From booking to your account

Every dollar is accounted for before the patient walks in. No ambiguity at any point in the transaction.

1

Patient books

Patient selects your service, sees the guaranteed price, and pays. Funds leave their account immediately.

2

Escrow holds

Payment is held in escrow. Not in your account, not refunded. Secured until service is complete.

3

Care delivered

Patient presents Health Key. You scan or verify. Deliver the defined service. No scope questions.

4

Funds released

Service confirmed complete. Funds are released to you. Settlement hits your bank account.
