What Happens on Your Side

Your workflow when a patient books

Here's what you and your staff actually experience. No new systems to learn.

When a patient books

You get notified

You receive a notification with the patient's name, service booked, appointment time, and payment confirmation. Their intake responses are attached.

Payment is already collected and held in escrow.
When the patient arrives

Staff scans their QR code

The patient shows their Health Key on their phone. Your front desk scans it and sees: paid, confirmed, intake complete. No insurance check. No copay. No clipboard.

Screen shows: PAID — $0 due at point of care.
After the visit

Confirm and get paid

Mark the visit complete in your OpenDoc dashboard. Payment is released to your bank account within 3–5 business days. No claims. No billing.

Money in your account in under a week.

Quick Answers

Common questions

Does this replace my EHR?

No. Nothing changes about your existing systems. This adds a new capability to your website — that's it.

What does this cost me?

OpenDoc takes a small percentage of each transaction. Build it into your Cleared price — it replaces what you'd spend on billing and collections for that visit.

Can I change my prices?

Anytime. Update in your OpenDoc dashboard and the widget updates automatically. Existing purchases stay at the original price.

What if a patient cancels?

Full refund within 72 hours. After that, a cancellation fee applies. No-shows get a partial refund — you receive a portion for reserved time.
