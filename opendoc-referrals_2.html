How It Works

One link. That's the whole system.

Any provider on OpenDoc can generate referral links for the providers they refer patients to. PCPs refer to specialists. Specialists refer to imaging, PT, pain management, other specialists. It all works the same way — a link.

The Provider Receiving the Referral

You share a link with whoever refers to you

In your OpenDoc dashboard, generate a unique link for each provider who sends you patients. Send it to them once — they use it for every patient they refer your way.

opendoc.com/book/canton-ortho?ref=dr-patel-pcp
opendoc.com/book/canton-ortho?ref=canton-urgent
opendoc.com/book/canton-ortho?ref=dr-lee-sports

Works for any combination: PCP → specialist, specialist → specialist, specialist → imaging, surgeon → PT. Any provider on OpenDoc can receive referrals this way.

Quick Answers

Common questions

Does the referring provider need an OpenDoc account?

No. They just need the link. They text it to patients. No login, no setup, no training. They can also keep a printed QR card at their front desk.

Is this just for PCPs referring to specialists?

No. It works for any referral direction. PCP to specialist. Specialist to imaging. Surgeon to PT. Orthopedist to pain management. Cardiologist to vascular. Any provider on OpenDoc can receive referrals from any other provider.

What does the referring provider say to the patient?

"I'm referring you to Dr. Mitchell for an orthopedic consultation. I'm going to text you a link — you can see their availability and book right from your phone."

Does this cost the referring provider anything?

Nothing. The link is free. The referring provider's only role is to hand it to the patient.
