Quick Answers

Common questions

What happens if a patient rates me low?

They don't get the share link. Low ratings are captured privately in your dashboard so you can follow up directly. Nothing goes public. This protects your reputation and gives you a chance to make it right.

What counts as a "high" rating?

4 or 5 stars. Only patients who had a genuinely good experience receive the share text. You're only amplifying your best moments.

Does this cost me anything extra?

No. Ratings and patient sharing are built into OpenDoc. The same transaction fee applies whether the patient found you through your website, a referral, or a friend's link.

Can I see my ratings?

Yes. Your dashboard shows your average rating, rating distribution, and trends over time. Ratings are internal to OpenDoc — they're not published publicly unless you choose to display them.

Ratings and sharing are on by default

Every completed visit prompts a rating. High raters get the share text automatically. Manage settings in your OpenDoc dashboard.
