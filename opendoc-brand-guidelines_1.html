04
State System
The pulse changes form and color to communicate Health Key authorization state. Pulse = alive/authorized. Flatline = dead/not authorized. This maps directly to the Health Key's three states.

DECISION REQUIREDWaveform: pulse | Stroke: #F59E0B | Container bg: #451A03 | Surface: #FFFBEB
NOT AUTHORIZEDWaveform: flatline | Stroke: #EF4444 | Container bg: #450A0A | Surface: #FEF2F2

07
Don'ts
Don't rotate The mark must always be horizontal. The flatline anchors the reading.
Don't thin the stroke The 8.75% stroke ratio is calibrated. Thinner reads as a chart, not a mark.
Don't use gradients The mark is one solid color. Gradients violate the Health Key design principles.
Don't reduce opacity Full opacity at all times. The pulse is present or it's a flatline. No in-between.
Don't add peaks Single peak only. Multiple peaks become a generic heartbeat monitor.
Don't use off-brand colors Navy, white, or state colors only. No purple, blue, or arbitrary hues.
